Stanford STEAM

Stanford STEAM is a student group at Stanford University. The purpose of this STEAM chapter is to engage Stanford students in experiences that cultivate mutual appreciation of engineering/science and humanities. We aim to provide an inclusive community for exploring topics related to both academic cultures (e.g. digital humanities, physics and archaeology, computer science and music) through hands-on projects and educational programming.

STEAM facilitates activities that can be broadly categorized as general meetings, projects, and quarterly talks/workshops. These activities provide hands-on experience in combining the engineering/sciences with humanities and educatation for the Stanford community on related work in the world. The meetings and talks grant breadth for students to explore the multiple facets of art and STEM, and projects enable in-depth and hands-on learning into a topic of interest. Our overall activities bring together students from humanities, sciences/engineering, and various academic fields in intellectual harmony.
